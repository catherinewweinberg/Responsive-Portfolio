About Me

I am a web developer with six years of experience in data analytics. I received a B.A. in Liberal Studies from Limestone College and a certificate in full stack web development from The George Washington University College of Professional Studies. Web development skills include JavaScript, CSS, Node.js, jQuery, Bootstrap and SQL. You can check out some of my work in the portfolio section.

I recently moved to the D.C. area from Scott Air Force Base (just outside of St. Louis, Missouri). My husband, Matt, is a c-17 pilot with the United States Air Force and we have a three year old chocolate lab named Moose. In my spare time I enjoy crossFit, running, and spending time with my family.
